Privacy Policy

App: Ninetales NoVeg Factory • Last updated: 2026-02-23

1. Overview

This app is an internal tool used by the owner to generate and upload short cooking videos. It is not a consumer-facing product.

2. Data we handle

  • OAuth tokens (access/refresh tokens) for connected social accounts, stored locally by the owner to enable automated posting.
  • Generated assets such as video files, thumbnails, subtitles, and captions stored on the owner’s device.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, job status, and error messages) to troubleshoot uploads.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with the connected platforms (e.g., TikTok/YouTube/Facebook) as required to upload and publish content.

4. Data retention and deletion

Tokens and logs are stored locally by the owner. To delete data, remove the local token files and generated output folders from the device.

5. Security

Token files should be kept private and stored on a secure device. Do not commit secrets to public repositories.
